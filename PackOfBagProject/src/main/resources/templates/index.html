MISSION

Pack A Bag For A Friend foundation is a non for profit charity that helps infants, children, youth at christmas by providing a high quality backpack of essentials (hats, gloves, socks, pajama's, book, toy, toothbrush). for the family, a complete festive food hamper is provided that includes: turkey, stuffing, gravy, potatoes, carrots, broccoli, celery, salad, crackers, milk, oranges, apples cookies and chocolate treat.

VISION

Our mission is to ensure that as many children and families in the Halton region as possible do not go without the basic clothing necessities and food during the Christmas season. We believe that every child deserves to experience the joy of the holidays, and we aim to provide essential items that foster warmth, comfort, and a sense of belonging.

What We Offer

Empowering Lives

We aim to empower individuals and families to overcome challenges and achieve their goals.

What You Find in a “Pack a Bag for a Friend” BackPack

Each backpack contains one each of the following, age and gender appropriate high-quality items:

  • Pajamas
  • Socks
  • Gloves or Mitts
  • Hat/Toque
  • Age appropriate toy/Gift Card (when possible)
  • Various book types: reading, activity, cookbooks, etc.
