WHO WE ARE

  • We are a registered 781218326RR0001 non-profit charity located in the Halton Region & able to provide income tax receipts for all monetary donations.
  • We are a "well-seasoned board" of 6 members incorporating approximately 100 years of business and guest experience amongst us.
  • We are passionate about genuinely helping those in need.
  • Our charity welcomes approximately 40-50 volunteers of all ages.
  • We strive to continually evolve.

WHO WE HELP

  • In December 2022, approximately 2,250 back packs were distributed to the following organizations:
  • Big Brothers and Big Sisters of Milton and Halton
  • St. Vincent de Paul
  • The families of Food4Kids Halton in Milton, Acton, Georgetown, Oakville, and Burlington
  • Milton Transitional House
  • Grace Anglican Church
  • Food for Life
  • Milton Community Living
  • Community Youth Program
  • Crossing Church
  • Living Hope for Care and Counseling
  • Shiafra Homes
  • Bob Rumball Canadian Centre of Excellence for the Deaf - Milton
  • Plus individuals in the community
